Plain-English Security

How Arsenal Vault Protects Your Data

Your firearms inventory is personal. Arsenal Vault is built so your records are separated, encrypted, and protected without making you become a cybersecurity expert.

The short version

Arsenal Vault keeps each customer in their own encrypted vault, protects sign-ins with modern account security, and limits what we collect to what the service needs to work. In plain terms: you get your own encrypted vault for your collection. We do not sell your data, we do not use your inventory for ads, and your collection stays yours.

Separate vault

Your records live in your own isolated database, not mixed into one giant shared pile.

Encrypted records

Sensitive inventory data is protected with SQLCipher and a unique 256-bit key.

Safer sign-in

Email verification, strong passwords, rate limits, and multi-factor authentication help keep strangers out.

Your own encrypted vault

Think of it like a safe deposit box. Your inventory is stored separately from other customers, and the vault itself is encrypted. If someone cannot open that vault, the contents are not readable in a useful way.

A unique 256-bit key

Each vault is protected with its own strong encryption key. That means one account's protection is not reused for everyone else. It is the digital version of changing the lock for every customer.

Account protections that matter

New accounts verify their email address. Passwords must be strong and are stored as one-way hashes, not plain text. Sign-in attempts are rate-limited, and multi-factor authentication gives you another layer of defense.

Backups without being careless

Backups are there so a technical problem does not become a personal disaster. They are handled with the same security mindset as the live vault instead of being treated like disposable files.

What we do not do

Security is not only locks and encryption. It is also respecting the fact that your collection data is private.

We do not sell your data to advertisers, brokers, or third parties.

We do not use your inventory for ads or profiling.

We do not make your collection public or searchable by other users.

A real-world note about security

No app can honestly promise that nothing bad could ever happen. What Arsenal Vault can promise is that we build with security in mind, use sensible modern protections, and keep improving as the product grows.

You can help too: use a unique password, turn on multi-factor authentication, keep your email account secure, and avoid sharing your login with anyone.
