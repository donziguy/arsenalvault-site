Security

Security for records you cannot replace

These records can include serials, values, locations, and photos. Arsenal Vault treats that like sensitive documentation, not casual app data.

The short version

Most apps put everyone's data in one database with per-user rows. Arsenal Vault is different: each owner gets a physically separate, encrypted database file, protected with its own unique 256-bit key.

The key that encrypts your database is itself encrypted with your personal key — envelope encryption. If one user's key is ever compromised, zero other accounts are affected.

What we do not do

  • We do not use your inventory for ads or profiling.
  • We do not make your collection public or searchable by other users.
