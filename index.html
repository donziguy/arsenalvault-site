Why this exists

Built after a collection was stolen

Arsenal Vault wasn't designed in a boardroom. When my dad's collection was stolen, I learned firsthand how valuable proper documentation is. Providing accurate information to the authorities and insurance companies without detailed records was a nightmare — serial numbers, purchase dates, photos, values. All the things you don't think about until you need them.

It's the system I wished I'd had. After building it for myself, I realized other collectors might find it useful too. So here we are.
