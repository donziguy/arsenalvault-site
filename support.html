Getting started

How do I get started?

Create your account on the cloud app and start documenting your collection in minutes. Prefer offline? Download the Windows desktop app from the download page.

Can I import data from other software?

Yes. Arsenal Vault supports CSV import for easy migration from spreadsheets and other inventory apps.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — 15 days free, no credit card required. You're only asked for payment if you continue after the trial.

Features & usage

How do I add photos?

When viewing or editing any item, open the Photos tab and use the upload button. Add multiple photos per item; they're automatically resized for storage.

Can I generate insurance reports?

Yes. Go to Reports > Collection Overview to generate a comprehensive PDF with photos, serial numbers, and values — ideal for insurance documentation.

What are the 15 parts categories?

Barrels, Triggers, Stocks & Grips, Optics & Sights, Magazines, Muzzle Devices, Handguards & Rails, Bolts & Carriers, Lowers & Receivers, Uppers, Springs & Pins, Lights & Lasers, Furniture, Suppressors, and Other Parts.

Security & privacy

Is my data secure?

Yes. Each cloud owner gets a separate SQLCipher-encrypted database with a unique 256-bit key, and sensitive fields like serial numbers get an additional encryption layer. Desktop users keep all data on their local machine.

Do you share or sell my data?

Never. Your inventory is yours alone. We don't share, sell, or analyze your collection data for any purpose. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I back up my data?

Cloud accounts have automatic daily backups with the ability to download your data anytime. Desktop users can create a complete backup from Admin > Backup.
