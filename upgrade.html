Common Questions

What happens after I buy?

You'll receive a license key via email immediately. Download the installer from the Downloads page, run it, and enter your key on the activation screen. That's it — you're in.

Is it truly offline?

Yes. After the one-time activation (requires internet), the app runs 100% offline. Your data never leaves your machine. All inventory is stored in a locally encrypted SQLCipher database on your computer.

What does "annual" mean exactly?

Your license is valid for one year from purchase and includes all updates released during that period. After a year, the app continues to work — you just won't receive new updates until you renew.

Can I move my license to a new computer?

Yes. One license covers one computer at a time. Contact support@arsenalvault.com to transfer your activation to a new machine.

What's the difference between Desktop and Cloud?

Desktop is local-only — one machine, no cloud sync, maximum privacy. Cloud gives you access from any browser, vault sharing with family/members, and automatic backups. Not sure? Compare plans →
