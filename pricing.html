Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. Sign up and get 14 days free with no credit card required. You'll only be asked for payment if you choose to continue after the trial.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes, anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

What are vault members?

Trusted people you invite to view or help manage your collection — a co-owner, spouse, or family member. The owner's plan covers all members.

What's your refund policy?

A 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. Not satisfied for any reason? Contact us for a full refund, no questions asked.

Cloud vs. desktop?

Cloud plans are subscription-based and work from any browser on any device. Desktop is a Windows app that runs entirely on your machine — no internet required, data never leaves your computer.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards via Stripe secure checkout.
