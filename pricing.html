Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card to start the free trial?

No. Sign up and get 14 days free with no credit card required. You'll only be asked for payment if you choose to continue after the trial.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

What are Vault Members?

Vault Members are trusted people you invite to view or help manage your collection — a co-owner, spouse, or trusted family member. The owner's plan covers all members.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest using per-user encryption keys and in transit via TLS. We never share your information with third parties. Your data. Your control. Your vault.

What's the difference between Cloud and Desktop?

Cloud plans are subscription-based and accessible from any browser on any device. Desktop is a Windows app that runs entirely on your machine — no internet required, data never leaves your computer.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards via Stripe Secure Checkout.
